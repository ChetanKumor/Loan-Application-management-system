Vehicle Loan

Overview

A vehicle loan is a financial product designed to help you purchase a new or used vehicle. The vehicle serves as collateral for the loan, which means the lender can repossess it if you fail to make payments.

Key Features:

  • Competitive interest rates starting from 7.25% p.a.
  • Loan amount up to 100% of on-road price for select models
  • Flexible repayment tenure of up to 7 years
  • Quick processing and approval within 24-48 hours
  • Minimal documentation
  • No hidden charges

Benefits:

  • Special rates for existing customers
  • Option to choose between fixed and floating interest rates
  • Balance transfer facility available
  • No prepayment penalties after 6 months
  • Special offers during festive seasons

Required Documents

Identity Proof (Any one):

  • Passport
  • Voter ID Card
  • Driving License
  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card

Address Proof (Any one):

  • Passport
  • Electricity Bill (not older than 3 months)
  • Telephone Bill (not older than 3 months)
  • Water Bill (not older than 3 months)
  • Aadhaar Card

Income Proof:

  • For Salaried Individuals:
    • Last 3 months' salary slips
    • Form 16 for the last year
    • Bank statements for the last 3 months
  • For Self-Employed/Business Owners:
    • Income Tax Returns for the last 2 years
    • Business financial statements
    • Bank statements for the last 6 months
    • GST returns (if applicable)

Vehicle Documents:

  • Proforma invoice from the dealer
  • Registration certificate (for used vehicles)
  • Insurance documents
  • Quotation for the vehicle

Loan Eligibility

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Age: 21 to 65 years at loan maturity
  • Income: Minimum monthly income of ₹20,000
  • Employment: Salaried individuals with at least 1 year of work experience or self-employed professionals with at least 2 years of business stability
  • Credit Score: 650 or above

Loan Amount:

The maximum loan amount you can avail depends on your repayment capacity and the vehicle's value. Generally, you can get up to 100% of the on-road price for new vehicles and up to 80% for used vehicles.

Loan Tenure:

The repayment tenure can range from 1 to 7 years, depending on your age, income, and the vehicle type.

! Important Note !

  • The interest rate and loan amount are subject to change based on the applicant's profile, vehicle type, and market conditions.
  • Processing fee of 0.5% to 1% of the loan amount is applicable.
  • Prepayment charges may apply for loans foreclosed within 6 months.
  • The bank reserves the right to reject any application without providing a reason.
  • Comprehensive vehicle insurance is mandatory for the entire loan tenure.
  • All documents submitted must be self-attested.
  • The vehicle will be hypothecated to the bank until the loan is fully repaid.

Helpful Tips

Before Applying:

  • Check and improve your credit score
  • Calculate your eligibility and EMI using our loan calculator
  • Compare interest rates and terms from different lenders
  • Research the vehicle thoroughly
  • Consider the total cost of ownership, including insurance, maintenance, and fuel

During Application:

  • Provide accurate information to avoid rejection
  • Negotiate for better interest rates
  • Read all terms and conditions carefully
  • Ask about hidden charges
  • Consider making a higher down payment to reduce EMI

After Approval:

  • Set up automatic payments to avoid missing EMIs
  • Keep track of your loan statement regularly
  • Consider making prepayments when you have surplus funds
  • Save all loan-related documents
  • Maintain the vehicle properly to preserve its value
