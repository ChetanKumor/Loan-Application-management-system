Personal Loans

Overview

A personal loan is an unsecured loan that provides a lump sum of money to be repaid in fixed monthly installments over a set period. Unlike secured loans, personal loans don't require collateral, making them accessible to a wider range of borrowers.

Key Features:

  • Loan amount: ₹10,000 to ₹50,00,000
  • Interest rates: Starting from 10.50% p.a.
  • Tenure: 12 to 60 months
  • Processing fee: Up to 2.5% of the loan amount
  • No collateral required
  • Minimal documentation
  • Quick approval and disbursement

Benefits:

  • Flexibility in end-use (no restrictions on how you use the funds)
  • Fixed EMIs for better financial planning
  • No need to pledge assets as security
  • Helps build credit history when repaid on time
  • Tax benefits available for specific uses (e.g., home renovation)

Eligibility Criteria

Age:

  • Minimum: 21 years
  • Maximum: 60 years at loan maturity

Income:

  • Salaried individuals: Minimum monthly income of ₹20,000
  • Self-employed professionals: Minimum annual income of ₹3,00,000
  • Self-employed non-professionals: Minimum annual income of ₹5,00,000

Employment:

  • Salaried: Minimum 2 years of total work experience with at least 1 year with the current employer
  • Self-employed: Business vintage of at least 3 years

Credit Score:

  • Minimum CIBIL score of 700+ for best rates
  • Loans may be available for scores between 650-700 at higher interest rates

Residence:

  • Indian resident
  • Stable residential address (minimum 1 year at current address)

Required Documents

Identity Proof (Any one):

  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Passport
  • Voter ID Card
  • Driving License

Address Proof (Any one):

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Passport
  • Voter ID Card
  • Driving License
  • Utility bills (not older than 3 months)
  • Rent Agreement

Income Proof:

For Salaried Individuals:

  • Last 3 months' salary slips
  • Form 16 or Income Tax Returns for the last 2 years
  • Bank statements for the last 6 months (showing salary credits)

For Self-Employed Individuals:

  • Income Tax Returns for the last 2 years
  • Business registration proof
  • Bank statements for the last 6 months
  • Profit & Loss account and Balance Sheet for the last 2 years (certified by CA)
  • GST registration (if applicable)

Additional Documents:

  • Passport-sized photographs
  • Proof of business existence (for self-employed)
  • Professional degree certificates (for self-employed professionals)

Loan Amount & Interest Rate

Loan Amount:

  • Minimum: ₹10,000
  • Maximum: Up to ₹50,00,000 (depending on income and eligibility)

Interest Rate:

  • Starting from 10.50% p.a.
  • Can go up to 24% p.a. based on profile and credit score
  • Fixed and floating rate options available

Factors Affecting Interest Rate:

  • Credit score
  • Income level
  • Employment type and stability
  • Relationship with the bank
  • Loan amount and tenure
  • Existing liabilities

Processing Fee:

  • Up to 2.5% of the loan amount
  • Minimum: ₹1,000
  • Maximum: ₹20,000

Other Charges:

  • Prepayment/Foreclosure charges: Up to 5% of the outstanding amount
  • Part-payment charges: Up to 5% of the part-payment amount
  • Late payment charges: 2-3% of the EMI amount
  • Documentation charges: ₹500-1,000
  • GST applicable on all fees and charges

Repayment Options

Tenure:

  • Minimum: 12 months (1 year)
  • Maximum: 60 months (5 years)

EMI Payment Modes:

  • Post-dated cheques (PDCs)
  • Electronic Clearing Service (ECS) mandate
  • Standing instructions on bank account
  • National Automated Clearing House (NACH) mandate
  • Online payment through net banking
  • Mobile banking apps

Prepayment Options:

  • Full prepayment (foreclosure) allowed after 6-12 months
  • Partial prepayment allowed (minimum 2 EMIs)
  • Prepayment charges may apply as per the terms

Default Consequences:

  • Late payment fees
  • Negative impact on credit score
  • Legal notices and recovery proceedings
  • Reporting to credit bureaus as defaulter

Application Process

Online Application:

  1. Visit our website and navigate to the Personal Loan section
  2. Fill in the basic details in the application form
  3. Upload the required documents
  4. Submit the application
  5. Receive acknowledgment and application reference number

Offline Application:

  1. Visit the nearest branch
  2. Collect the personal loan application form
  3. Fill in the required details
  4. Submit the form along with the necessary documents
  5. Receive acknowledgment and application reference number

Processing Timeline:

  • Initial assessment: 1-2 working days
  • Verification and approval: 2-5 working days
  • Disbursement: 1-2 working days after approval
  • Total time: Typically 3-7 working days

Verification Process:

  • Document verification
  • Credit bureau check
  • Income and employment verification
  • Residence verification (may include physical visit)
  • Telephonic verification
