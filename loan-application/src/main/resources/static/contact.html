Frequently Asked Questions

How does SVA make money?

SVA makes money through advertising and referral fees from our financial partners when a user applies for a product through our platform. This allows us to provide our comparison services to you for free. Rest assured, our recommendations are always based on what's best for you, not our partners.

Is my information secure with SVA?

Yes, we take data security very seriously. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal information. We never sell your data to third parties without your explicit consent.

How do I know I'm getting the best rates?

We work with hundreds of lenders and financial institutions to ensure you have access to a wide range of options. Our comparison tools show you real-time rates based on your specific financial situation, allowing you to make an informed decision.

Can I apply for multiple products to compare offers?

Yes, you can apply for multiple products through our platform. However, keep in mind that some financial products may require a hard credit check, which can temporarily impact your credit score. Our platform will clearly indicate when a hard credit check is required.

How quickly will I receive offers after applying?

For many products, you'll receive offers instantly or within minutes. For more complex financial products like mortgages or business loans, it may take 1-2 business days to receive all available offers.
