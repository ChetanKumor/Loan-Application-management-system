Business Loan

Overview

A business loan is a financial product designed to help entrepreneurs and business owners fund their business operations, expansion, equipment purchase, working capital needs, or other business-related expenses.

Key Features:

  • Competitive interest rates starting from 10.50% p.a.
  • Loan amount ranging from ₹5 lakhs to ₹5 crores
  • Flexible repayment tenure of up to 10 years
  • Quick processing and approval within 3-7 business days
  • Minimal documentation for existing businesses
  • No collateral required for loans up to ₹50 lakhs (for eligible businesses)

Benefits:

  • Tax benefits on interest paid under Section 37(1) of Income Tax Act
  • Customized repayment options based on business cash flow
  • Overdraft facility available for better fund management
  • Special rates for existing customers
  • Dedicated relationship manager for loan amounts above ₹50 lakhs

Required Documents

Business Documents:

  • Business registration documents (Certificate of Incorporation, Partnership Deed, etc.)
  • Business license/permits
  • GST registration certificate
  • Shop and establishment certificate
  • MSME registration (if applicable)
  • Business address proof

Financial Documents:

  • Last 2-3 years' audited financial statements
  • Last 2-3 years' Income Tax Returns
  • Last 6-12 months' bank statements
  • GST returns for the last year
  • Projected financials (for new businesses or expansion projects)
  • Business plan (for new businesses)

Personal Documents:

  • Identity proof of directors/partners/proprietor
  • Address proof of directors/partners/proprietor
  • PAN card of the business and directors/partners/proprietor
  • Personal Income Tax Returns for the last 2 years
  • KYC documents of directors/partners/proprietor

Collateral Documents (if applicable):

  • Property documents
  • Valuation report of the property
  • Fixed deposit receipts
  • Other security documents

Loan Eligibility

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Business Age: Minimum 2 years of business operation (1 year for select industries)
  • Annual Turnover: Minimum annual turnover of ₹40 lakhs
  • Profitability: Business should be profitable for at least 1 year
  • Credit Score: Business or promoter credit score of 700 or above
  • Age of Applicant: 25 to 65 years

Loan Amount:

The maximum loan amount you can avail depends on your business turnover, profitability, and repayment capacity. Generally, you can get up to 20% of your annual turnover as a business loan.

Loan Tenure:

The repayment tenure can range from 1 to 10 years, depending on the loan amount, purpose, and business cash flow.

! Important Note !

  • The interest rate and loan amount are subject to change based on the business profile, industry, and market conditions.
  • Processing fee of 1% to 2% of the loan amount is applicable.
  • Prepayment charges may apply for loans foreclosed within 1 year.
  • The bank reserves the right to reject any application without providing a reason.
  • Business insurance may be required for certain loan amounts.
  • All documents submitted must be self-attested.
  • The bank may request additional documents during the verification process.
  • For collateral-free loans, a personal guarantee from directors/partners/proprietor is mandatory.

Helpful Tips

Before Applying:

  • Ensure your business and personal credit scores are good
  • Organize your financial statements and business documents
  • Calculate your debt service coverage ratio (DSCR)
  • Prepare a detailed business plan if applying for a large loan
  • Clear any existing tax dues or business liabilities

During Application:

  • Be transparent about your business financials
  • Provide accurate information to avoid rejection
  • Negotiate for better interest rates and terms
  • Read all terms and conditions carefully
  • Ask about hidden charges and prepayment options

After Approval:

  • Set up automatic payments to avoid missing EMIs
  • Keep track of your loan statement regularly
  • Maintain good business cash flow
  • Consider making prepayments when you have surplus funds
  • Save all loan-related documents
