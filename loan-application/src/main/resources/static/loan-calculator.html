Understanding Your Loan Calculation

Our loan calculator helps you estimate your monthly payments and the total cost of your loan. Here's what each term means:

Monthly Payment

The amount you'll pay each month, including principal and interest.

Total Payment

The total amount you'll pay over the life of the loan, including principal and interest.

Total Interest

The total amount of interest you'll pay over the life of the loan.
