Mortgage Loan

Overview

A mortgage loan is a secured loan where you pledge your existing property as collateral to obtain funds for various purposes such as home renovation, education, business expansion, or other financial needs.

Key Features:

  • Competitive interest rates starting from 7.00% p.a.
  • Loan amount up to 70% of the property value
  • Flexible repayment tenure of up to 20 years
  • Quick processing and approval within 3-5 business days
  • Minimal documentation
  • No restrictions on end-use of funds (except for speculative purposes)

Benefits:

  • Lower interest rates compared to personal loans
  • Tax benefits available if used for home renovation or construction
  • Option to choose between fixed and floating interest rates
  • Overdraft facility available for better fund management
  • No prepayment penalties on floating rate loans

Required Documents

Identity Proof (Any one):

  • Passport
  • Voter ID Card
  • Driving License
  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card

Address Proof (Any one):

  • Passport
  • Electricity Bill (not older than 3 months)
  • Telephone Bill (not older than 3 months)
  • Water Bill (not older than 3 months)
  • Aadhaar Card

Income Proof:

  • For Salaried Individuals:
    • Last 3 months' salary slips
    • Form 16 for the last 2 years
    • Bank statements for the last 6 months
  • For Self-Employed/Business Owners:
    • Income Tax Returns for the last 2 years
    • Business financial statements
    • Bank statements for the last 6 months
    • GST returns (if applicable)

Property Documents:

  • Original property deed/title documents
  • Property tax receipts
  • NOC from housing society/builder
  • Approved building plan
  • Chain of property ownership documents
  • Property valuation report
  • Property insurance documents

Loan Eligibility

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Age: 21 to 70 years at loan maturity
  • Income: Minimum monthly income of ₹30,000
  • Employment: Salaried individuals with at least 2 years of work experience or self-employed professionals with at least 3 years of business stability
  • Credit Score: 700 or above
  • Property: Residential or commercial property in good condition with clear title

Loan Amount:

The maximum loan amount you can avail depends on your repayment capacity and the value of your property. Generally, you can get up to 70% of the property value as a loan.

Loan Tenure:

The repayment tenure can range from 5 to 20 years, depending on your age and repayment capacity.

! Important Note !

  • The interest rate and loan amount are subject to change based on the applicant's profile and market conditions.
  • Processing fee of 0.5% to 1% of the loan amount is applicable.
  • Legal and technical assessment charges are applicable.
  • Prepayment charges may apply for fixed-rate loans.
  • The bank reserves the right to reject any application without providing a reason.
  • Property insurance is mandatory for the entire loan tenure.
  • The bank will place a lien on your property until the loan is fully repaid.
  • In case of default, the bank has the right to seize and sell the mortgaged property.

Helpful Tips

Before Applying:

  • Check and improve your credit score
  • Get your property documents in order
  • Compare interest rates and terms from different lenders
  • Calculate your eligibility and EMI using our loan calculator
  • Ensure your property has a clear title with no disputes

During Application:

  • Be transparent about your financial situation
  • Read all terms and conditions carefully
  • Understand the foreclosure terms and charges
  • Negotiate for better interest rates
  • Ask about hidden charges

After Approval:

  • Set up automatic payments to avoid missing EMIs
  • Keep track of your loan statement regularly
  • Consider making prepayments when you have surplus funds
  • Keep all loan-related documents safe
  • Inform the bank if there are any changes to the property status
